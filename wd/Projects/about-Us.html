ABOUT US

The hamro Sneaker Nepal is the one of the rising company of Nepal .It was established in 2010 BS by the young and energetic group of team.This company is regarded as one of the best new conceptual company in context of Nepal. We are a young start-up from Nepal, created to provide quality and innovation in the footwear industry. We are a branding and fashion company, we want to create brand value in an original and different way. We want to offer a completely new marketing solution to be different and recognizable everywhere. Our focus? Make people feel good with your customized shoes. A fresh way to introduce your brand!We are a young, innovative and dynamic team with long experience in the footwear industry, and we’re offering a new and original product. Designed in Nepal and created in collaboration with local manufacturers that allows a domestic production. The “Made in Nepal” guaranteed high quality, we use the best materials and work with Spanish craftsmen recognized worldwide.

Here at Hamro Sneaker, we are all about… you guessed it… sneakers. We intend to cater to the curiosity of sneakers collectors, consumers, and enthusiasts alike. As sneakers enthusiasts ourselves, we are driven by the passion to provide a platform where you can learn, be entertained, and stay current pertaining to all things related to this genre of footwear. We forsee Sneaker Swarm becoming the “hive” for all things sneaker.At Sneaker Swarm, footwear is kind of a BIG DEAL. This means you can count on us to contribute to sneaker culture in a unique and unprecedented way. We aim to influence how we wear, when we wear, and who wears these rubber-soled works of art. Sneaker Swarm will go deeper into the sneaker culture by featuring interviews, videos, photo shoots, contests, and thought-provoking articles that examine the impact of this footwear on the society in which we create and share.
